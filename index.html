Building Multiplayer WebGL

Hole.io is a fantastic multiplayer .io game in which you control a black hole and must travel around the city absorbing everything on your path. You will start with a small hole, absorb small items such as post boxes, street lights and even people to grow the size of your hole.
SHOW MORE

You are racing against the time limit and other players in this game as the more that you absorb and the larger your hole gets the more points you will have obtained. You can even absorb smaller players than yourself so pick a target and absorb them all. Have fun!

Release Date

The game was initially released on mobile platforms (iOS and Android) in June 2018. The online version has been available since July 2018.

Developer

Hole.io was made by Voodoo, the same developer who created the fantastic .io game Paper.io.
